You are invited to consider joining a clinical research study about heart failure with reduced ejection fraction (HFrEF).

This study is testing dapagliflozin compared with placebo. The study is designed to see whether dapagliflozin can improve exercise capacity and symptoms in adults with HFrEF.

If you join, the research team will explain what study visits, questionnaires, walking assessments, and other study procedures are involved. The team will also explain possible risks, possible benefits, alternatives to joining, and your rights as a participant.

Taking part is your choice. You may ask questions at any time before deciding whether to join. If you start the study and later want to stop, you may stop participating at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.
